Introduction

Uganda is gifted by nature and has a variety of Mountains namely Mt.Elgon,Mt.Rwenzori,Mt. Muhabura, Mt Moroto to mention but a few.

Mt. Elgon which is located at the border of Uganda and Kenya is the oldest and largest solitary ,volcanic mountain in Uganda

Mt. Elgon has a national park which a home to over 300 species of birds including the endangered lammergeyer

Mt Elgon offers tourists a wonderful experience of hiking and climbing the mountain, Nature Walks, Birding and Cave Exploration
